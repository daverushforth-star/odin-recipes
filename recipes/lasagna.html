Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • White sauce
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Minced Beef
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Brown mince in frying pan
  2. Mix in tomato sauce and wait til cooked
  3. In a glass dish layer mince with some white sauce and a pasta sheet. Repeat as necessary
  4. Grate parmesan on top
  5. Bake in oven until browned off
